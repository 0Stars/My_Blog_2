review on the movies

Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone
NOTE: There is a spoiler in the description.

One of my favorite movies and even if you haven't read the book, it's still a great movie. Even though it was a little scary with (SPOILER ALERT) Lord Voldemort trying to kill 11 year old Harry, it was still great. Definitely going to watch again if I have time.

Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets
NOTE: There is a spoiler in the description.

Before watching this movie, you should watch the previous movie (Harry Potter and the Philospher's Stone) or you'll miss some of the fun. This is one of the best movies I've watched. But, (SPOILER ALERT) when Harry, Ron and Professor Lockhart go into the chamber, it gets scary. But, it is funny to me when Professor Lockhart's spell backfires and erases his memory instead of Harry and Ron's memories.
